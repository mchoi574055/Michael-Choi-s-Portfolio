Projects

YuGiOh Meta Card Prediction using DeBERTa

Yu-Gi-Oh, a trading card game, has over 12,000 different cards printed in its 25-year history, but not all are suitable for competitive play.

This project was undertaken to predict a card's competitive viability using its specific attributes. A pre-trained transformer-based NLP model, DeBERTa, was utilized to understand the complexities of the game's effects. The model's goal was to classify cards as either suitable (1) or unsuitable (0) for competitive play, providing insight into what makes a Yu-Gi-Oh card successful in the game's meta.
